my fav sport

Table tennis (also known as ping-pong or whiff-whaff) is a
racket sport derived from tennis but distinguished by its
playing surface being atop a stationary table, rather than
the court on which players stand. Either individually or in
teams of two, players take alternating turns returning a
light, hollow ball over the table's net onto the opposing
half of the court using small rackets until they fail to do so,
which results in a point for the opponent.
